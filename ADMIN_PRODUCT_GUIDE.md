# Admin Product Management Guide

## How to Add Products

### Step 1: Login to Admin Dashboard
1. Go to the admin dashboard
2. Log in with your admin account
3. Navigate to **Products Management**

### Step 2: Click "Add Product" Button
Click the blue **"Add Product"** button in the top right corner

### Step 3: Fill in Product Details

#### Required Fields (marked with *)
- **Product Name**: Enter the product name (e.g., "Premium Leather Journal")
- **Description**: Write a detailed product description
- **Price**: Enter the price in dollars (must be greater than 0)
- **Category**: Select from available categories
- **Stock Quantity**: Enter how many items are in stock

#### Optional Fields
- **Discount**: Enter discount percentage (0-100%)
- **Image URL**: Paste a product image URL (will show preview)

### Step 4: Preview Image
Once you paste an image URL, you'll see a preview of the image that will be displayed to customers

### Step 5: Submit
Click **"Create Product"** to save the product

---

## How to Edit Products

1. Go to **Products Management**
2. Find the product you want to edit in the list
3. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
4. Make your changes in the form
5. Click **"Update Product"** to save

---

## How to Delete Products

1. Go to **Products Management**
2. Find the product you want to delete
3. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

---

## Product Features

### Stock Status Badge
- **Green**: Product is in stock
- **Yellow**: Low stock (less than 5 items)
- **Red**: Out of stock

### Discount Badge
Shows the discount percentage in orange if a discount is applied

### Pricing Display
- Shows original price in bold
- Shows discount percentage if applied
- Customers see discounted price in the store

---

## Image Guidelines

- Use image URLs from:
  - Unsplash (https://unsplash.com)
  - Pexels (https://pexels.com)
  - Pixabay (https://pixabay.com)
  - Your own image hosting service

- Recommended image size: 400x400 pixels or larger
- Formats: JPG, PNG, WebP

---

## Product Information Tips

### Product Name
- Keep it clear and descriptive
- Include key features if relevant
- Example: "Premium Leather Journal with 200 Pages"

### Description
- Write 2-3 sentences about the product
- Mention key features and benefits
- Example: "Handcrafted leather journal with 200 pages of premium paper. Perfect for journaling, sketching, or note-taking. Includes ribbon bookmark."

### Price
- Enter the base price
- Discounts are applied on top of the price
- Example: Base price $29.99, Discount 10% = Customer pays $26.99

### Stock
- Update after sales to keep accurate
- System won't let customers buy if stock is 0
- Yellow badge shows when less than 5 items

## Troubleshooting

### Product Not Appearing in Store
- Check that all **required fields** are filled
- Verify the **category** is selected
- Check the **price** is greater than 0
- Ensure **stock** is set to at least 1

### Image Not Loading
- Check the image URL is valid and accessible
- Try a different image URL
- Use Unsplash or similar service for reliable images

### Discount Not Showing
- Enter discount percentage (1-100)
- Discount must be greater than 0
- Check customer sees the "OFF" badge on the product
